I am passionate about architecting digital spaces; constantly experimenting with projects of varying scales ranging from an app to a product. My previous specialization is in Architecture and I translate my knowledge of designing for physical realm (personal and public spaces) in my current practice in UX.

My design process

My design decisions are driven by extensive research, analytics, constant iteration and user testing. I am an advocate for designing using thick data ( learning about WHY people do what they do ) and not limiting design decisions with big data.
