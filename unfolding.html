Unfolding

Interactive Product Design: Exploration of IOT to personalize art!

IDEA

01

Personalization is becoming a strategic differentiator for the success of a company or a product.But the purpose of art has always been to communicate ideas, politically,spiritually or philosophically; or to state an opinion to a larger audience. I started thinking what if art can personalize its message to a particular individual?

But can the interaction be directed to a paticular person in the crowd?

Personalization in Art !

CONCEPT

02

What If ???
This direction of thinking opened up a plethora of possibilities to explore in the field of personalized interactions with a physical medium.
I wanted to work with this metaphor. Using image sampler in Grasshopper (Visual programming tool based on python) I generated the pattern that would constitute the model. The next step was finding a way to randomize the pattern and revealing it only through a secret code.

Node based Algorithm

PROCESS

03

Initially I inverted the image (in grey scale) to obtain a larger range of intensity values that can be recognized by the image sampler node in the software.
The geomerty of the image was obtained by controlling the scale of the circles that mapped the image. This geometry was translated to a physical model.

Dots

I taught myself how to code an Arduino through online tutorials and played with multiple sensors and RFID to figure the right method to gain this particular effect.



Step 1 : Segmentation

The frame is divided into five segments that is re-oriented to randomize the dots. The dots re-align itself with the support of servo motors. The angle of the motors are controlled by a micro-controller.



Step 2 : Wireless Communication

The RFID tag ensures that the art work is revealed only to the person who has the key to unlock it.
